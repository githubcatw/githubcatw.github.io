Here are 4 three.js tests I made. Each one improves over the previous one.
ball_bounce is the simplest, it just shows a simple animation.
wasd adds camera movement.
placer lets you place floating cubes.
Finally, spin (the most complete) lets you rotate the camera too. It suffers from control inversion.

Controls

WASD: Move camera (wasd and up).
IJKL: Rotate camera (spin and up).
Any mouse button: Add cube (placer and up).
